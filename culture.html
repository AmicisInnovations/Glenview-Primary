CULTURE

More Than a Performance: Cultivating Creativity, Connection, and Confidence for Life.

At Glenview Primary, our cultural activities are a vibrant thread in the fabric of our community. They are designed to unlock imagination, foster collaboration, and give every child a voice. We believe in the power of shared creative expression to build empathy, resilience, and the courage to share one's unique perspective with the world. Whether your child is taking their first bow or helping to build a masterpiece behind the scenes, they will discover a stage where they belong, a space to explore, and the confidence to let their light shine.

Line Dancing

Step into the fun with our Glenview Primary Line Dancing club! It's a fantastic way to get moving, improve coordination, and build confidence as we learn to move together as a group. Students don't need a partner, just a willingness to listen, follow the beat, and have a great time. We celebrate every step and every sequence mastered, making it a joyful and inclusive activity for everyone. So put on your boots and come join the line

Choir

Glenview Primary's choir is where every voice finds a home and every singer finds their confidence. We build harmony through fun, friendship, and the fundamentals of music, creating a joyful and inclusive space for all. Our focus is on the thrill of singing together, fostering teamwork and a shared sense of accomplishment. For us, the true success is measured not in solos, but in the smiles we share and the joy we raise together.
