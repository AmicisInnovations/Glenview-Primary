SPORTS

More Than a Game: Building Character, Community, and Champions for Life

At Glenview Primary, athletics are a vital part of a well-rounded education. Our programs are designed to develop not just skilled athletes, but resilient leaders, dedicated teammates, and disciplined students. We believe in the power of sport to teach perseverance, sportsmanship, and the joy of striving for a common goal. Whether your child is a seasoned competitor or trying a sport for the first time, they will find a place to belong, grow, and thrive.

Soccer

At Glenview Primary, our soccer program is all about kicking off a lifelong love for the game! We focus on fun, friendship, and fundamental skills, where every child is encouraged to be part of the team. Through inclusive games and positive coaching, we build confidence, sportsmanship, and happy, healthy players. It’s less about the scoreline and all about the smiles!

Netball

Our netball programme is a fantastic opportunity for students to develop teamwork, agility, and confidence. We focus on teaching the fundamentals of passing, shooting, and spatial awareness in a fun, supportive, and inclusive environment. Whether they are new to the sport or building on their skills, every player is encouraged to do their best, support their teammates, and enjoy the thrill of the game. It’s a wonderful way to make new friends and stay active!

Athletics

At Glenview Primary, our athletics program is designed to spark a lasting enthusiasm for movement and sport! We celebrate personal effort, teamwork, and the joy of being active. Every child is empowered to discover their strength and speed in a positive, inclusive atmosphere. Our coaching focuses on building resilience, camaraderie, and a foundation for a healthy life. Here, the greatest achievement isn't just breaking a record—it's breaking a personal best with a beaming smile.

Chess

At Glenview Primary, our chess club is where young minds learn to love the classic challenge of the board! We nurture a passion for strategy, patience, and problem-solving in a supportive environment. Every student is welcomed into our community to learn, think, and grow together. Through engaging activities and guided discovery, we foster not just future players, but confident thinkers and gracious competitors. For us, the biggest win isn't capturing the king—it's the thrill of a plan well played.
