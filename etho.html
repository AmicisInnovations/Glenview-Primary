WHY CHOOSE GLENVIEW?

VISION & MISSION

Vision

To inspire confident, compassionate, and capable learners who are prepared for the future

Mission

At Glenview Primary, we nurture every child’s potential by providing quality education, fostering creativity, and building strong character in a safe and supportive environment.

The Glenview Lion

The Glenview Lion is a symbol of courage, strength, and unity. Just as a lion leads with confidence and protects its pride, it reminds us to be brave in learning, bold in character, and supportive of one another. It reflects our values of respect, resilience, and community, inspiring every learner to grow and lead with heart.
